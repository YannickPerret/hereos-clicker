# ITCH.IO — Page description

## Title
HereOS

## Tagline
Hack. Fight. Survive. A cyberpunk multiplayer clicker RPG in your browser.

## Short description (for listings)
HereOS is a browser-based cyberpunk clicker RPG. Click to hack, earn credits, gear up, and raid turn-based dungeons — alone or with other players in real time.

## Full description

```
In the neon-drenched sprawl of HereOS, you are a RUNNER — a rogue operative surviving by hacking, fighting, and adapting.

Click to breach terminals. Earn credits. Level up. Choose your spec. Dominate the leaderboard.

──────────────────────────────
⚡ CORE GAMEPLAY
──────────────────────────────

HereOS is a browser idle/clicker RPG with deep RPG mechanics layered on top. Hack terminals to earn credits, buy better gear, and watch your characters power scale across dozens of levels.

Offline earnings let you come back to passive progress — capped at 4 hours so you always have a reason to log back in.

──────────────────────────────
🧠 CHOOSE YOUR SPEC
──────────────────────────────

Four unique specializations, each with 4 signature combat skills (called "Programmes"):

▸ HACKER — Breach Protocol / Short Circuit / Backdoor / Kernel Panic
▸ NETRUNNER — Ping Scan / ICE Breaker / Neural Overload / System Restore
▸ SAMURAI — Wired Reflexes / Mono-Edge Slash / Subdermal Shield / Adrenaline Surge
▸ CHROME DEALER — Deploy Turret / Repair Nanobots / EMP Blast / Chrome Overcharge

──────────────────────────────
⚔️ DUNGEON COMBAT
──────────────────────────────

Turn-based combat with real depth:
- Solo runs or group raids with up to 4 players
- Turn rotation with AFK timeout and auto-attack
- Critical hits, DOTs, shields, stuns, buffs, heals
- Enemy loot drops with configurable chances
- Some floors require 2-3 players minimum
- Death penalty: lose 10% credits and respawn at 10 HP

──────────────────────────────
🌐 REAL MULTIPLAYER
──────────────────────────────

Form a party, set a countdown, and raid together — all in real time. Party chat auto-creates when you group up. The leader controls the run; members can be kicked (but not during an active dungeon).

──────────────────────────────
⚡ PVP
──────────────────────────────

Real-time matchmaking queue. Turn-based 1v1 combat. ELO ranking system (K=32). Your equipment, talents, and skills all factor into stats.

──────────────────────────────
📈 PROGRESSION SYSTEMS
──────────────────────────────

▸ Talent tree — unlock and invest points across tiers
▸ Companions — buy and level them up for passive bonuses
▸ Daily missions — 3 random objectives per day
▸ Quest chains — main and seasonal quest lines
▸ Leaderboard — compete globally for top ranking
▸ Daily rewards — 7-day reward cycle

──────────────────────────────
💬 COMMUNITY FEATURES
──────────────────────────────

IRC-style floating chat with public channels, private rooms (password-protected), and auto-generated party channels.

──────────────────────────────

No download. No install. Open your browser and start hacking.

→ Play now: https://www.hereos.yannickperret.com
```
